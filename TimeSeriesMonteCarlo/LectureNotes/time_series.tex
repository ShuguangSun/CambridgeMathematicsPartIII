\begin{defn}[Time Series]
  \label{defn:time_series:1}
  A set of observations $(X_{t})$, each being recorded at a predictable
  time $t \in T_{0}$.

  In a continuous time series, $T_{0}$ is continuous.  In a discrete
  time series, $T_{0}$ is discrete.
\end{defn}
