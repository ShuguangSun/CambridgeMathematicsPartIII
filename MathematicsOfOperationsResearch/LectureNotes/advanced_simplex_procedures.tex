\section{The Two-Phase Simplex Method}
\label{sec:two-phase-simplex}

Finding an initial \bfs is easy if the constraints have the form $Ax =
b$ where $b \geq 0$, as
\begin{equation}
  \label{eq:22}
  Ax + z = b, (x, z) = (0, b)
\end{equation}
